# Phase 14 Post-Launch Improvements

## Objective
Use real pilot feedback to drive the next implementation wave.

## Highest-Value Follow-Ups
- QR scanner and browser camera support
- pass product management and assignment
- richer branch-aware filtering across dashboard, members, and payments
- printable receipts
- better reports and date filtering
- staff role management and permission UI

## Performance Follow-Ups
- client-side caching for high-traffic operational pages
- reduce repeated Supabase reads on shared route transitions
- add measured route timing before deeper optimization work

## Product Follow-Ups
- member portal or member dashboard
- branch-level analytics
- custom branding upgrades
- online payments after manual flow is stable

## Success Review
Review after pilot:
- which workflows were used daily
- which workflows blocked front desk speed
- which metrics matter to owners in practice
- which permissions were too coarse
